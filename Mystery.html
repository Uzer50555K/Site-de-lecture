The Outsider

Author: Stephen King

Rating: 4.7/5

Synopsis: "The Outsider" by Stephen King is a gripping blend of crime thriller and supernatural horror. The story begins with the arrest of Terry Maitland, a respected teacher and little league coach, for the brutal murder of a young boy. As Detective Ralph Anderson investigates, he uncovers a perplexing case with conflicting evidence that challenges the town's perception of Terry. King skillfully weaves a narrative that explores the thin line between reality and the unexplainable, as dark forces come into play. The novel is a masterful exploration of the impact of fear, doubt, and the unknown on a small community, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking conclusion. King's trademark storytelling prowess is on full display in this thrilling and thought-provoking tale.

Review:Enthralling and suspenseful, The Outsider by Stephen King captivates with its seamless blend of crime and supernatural elements. The well-crafted characters and gripping plot make it a compelling read for fans of both mystery and horror genres.

The Silent Patient

Author:Alex Michaelides

Rating: 4.4/5

Synopsis: "The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides is a psychological thriller centered around Alicia Berenson, a celebrated painter who suddenly shoots her husband and then stops speaking. She becomes an enigmatic patient at a secure psychiatric facility. Theo Faber, a criminal psychotherapist, is determined to unravel the mystery behind her silence and understand the motive for the crime. As Theo delves into Alicia's past and therapy sessions, he discovers dark secrets that lead to a shocking and unexpected conclusion. The novel is known for its clever twists and psychological suspense.

Review:Riveting and mind-bending, "The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides captivated me from start to finish. The psychological twists kept me guessing, and the resolution left me stunned. A gripping thriller that lives up to its hype.
